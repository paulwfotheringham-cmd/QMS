Fifteen integrated modules

The same functional breadth called out on reference MedTech QMS product pages — expressed here as a clear module map for PRIA’s review.

Document Library

Central repository, search, and controlled viewing of approved content.

Document Control

Revisions, approvals, effective dates, and obsolescence.

Forms & Records

Structured data capture with status, routing, and retention.

Training

Role-based assignments, completion evidence, and overdue visibility.

Change Control

Planned changes, impact assessment, and implementation tracking.

Risk Management

Risk register, links to design and post-market inputs (e.g. ISO 14971 workflow).

Complaints

Intake, evaluation, regulatory reporting considerations, and closure.

CAPAs

Root cause, actions, effectiveness checks, and linkage to complaints and changes.

Internal Audit

Schedule, findings, and follow-up to management and CAPA.

Nonconforming Product

NCRs, disposition, and segregation/traceability hooks.

Management Review

Inputs, minutes, and quality system action items.

Supplier Control

Approved supplier list, evaluations, and purchasing constraints.

Preventive Maintenance

Equipment schedules, work orders, and downtime visibility.

Measuring Equipment

Calibration due dates, standards, and out-of-tolerance handling.

Products

Device/product records, specifications, and DHF/DMR index alignment.
